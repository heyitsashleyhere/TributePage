Chuck Palaniuk

From Charles to Chuck

"The things you own end up owning you."

  • 1962 - born in Pasco, Washington, USA
  • 1986 - graduated from the University of Oregon School of Journalism
  • 1988 - quit his job as a journalist
  • 1997 - won the Pacific Northwest Booksellers Association Award and Oregon Book Award for Best Novel for Fight Club
  • 1999 - nominated for Oregon Book Award for Best Novel for Lullaby
  • 2002 - nominated for Bram Stoker Award for Best Novel for Lullaby
  • 2002 - nominated for Bram Stoker Award for Best Novel for Lullaby
  • 2003 - won the Pacific Northwest Booksellers Association Award for Lullaby
  • 2005 - nominated for the Bram Stoker Award for Best Novel for Haunted
  • 2020 - his most recent novel The Invention of Sound is published
